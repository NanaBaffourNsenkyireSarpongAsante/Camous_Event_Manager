Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our events

How do I purchase tickets?

You can purchase tickets directly from our website by visiting the Tickets page, selecting your preferred event, and clicking the "Buy Now" button. Payment can be made via mobile money, credit card, or bank transfer. After successful payment, you'll receive a confirmation email with your e-ticket.

Can I get a refund?

Refunds are available up to 7 days before the event. Please contact our support team with your ticket reference number to process a refund. Some events may have different refund policies, so always check the event details before purchasing.

Are tickets transferable?

Yes, most tickets are transferable. You can transfer your ticket to someone else by logging into your account and using the "Transfer Ticket" option. The new attendee will receive an email with their updated e-ticket.

What time should I arrive?

We recommend arriving at least 30 minutes before the event starts to allow time for check-in and seating. For popular events, arriving earlier is advised. Check your ticket or the event page for specific door opening times.

Is there an age restriction?

Age restrictions vary by event. Check the specific event page for age requirements. Some events are all-ages, while others may be 18+ or 21+. Valid ID may be required for age-restricted events.

Can I take photos/videos?

Photography and video policies vary by event. Most events allow personal photos, but professional equipment may be restricted. Check the event guidelines or look for signage at the venue. Some performances may have strict no-photo policies.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including Mobile Money (MTN, Vodafone, AirtelTigo), Visa/Mastercard credit/debit cards, and bank transfers. All payments are processed securely through our payment partners.

Is my payment information secure?

Absolutely! We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your payment information. We never store your full payment details on our servers. All transactions are processed through secure, PCI-compliant payment gateways.

Do you offer group discounts?

Many events offer group discounts for parties of 5 or more. Contact our support team with your event of interest and group size, and we'll provide you with a custom quote and discount code.

How do I become an organizer?

If you're interested in organizing events on our platform, please visit our "Partner With Us" page or contact us through the email: partnerships@eventhub.com. We'd love to discuss partnership opportunities and help bring your event ideas to life.

What are the fees for organizers?

Our fee structure varies based on event type, size, and services required. Generally, we charge a small percentage of ticket sales plus a fixed fee per ticket. Contact our partnerships team for a detailed quote tailored to your event.

Is there parking available?

Most venues offer parking facilities. Check the specific event page for parking information. Some central locations may have limited parking, so we recommend using public transport when possible. Paid parking may apply at some venues.

What items are prohibited?

Prohibited items typically include weapons, illegal substances, outside food and beverages, large bags, and professional recording equipment. Check the event page or contact the venue for their specific prohibited items list.

Is the venue accessible?

We strive to make all events accessible. Most venues have wheelchair access, accessible restrooms, and special accommodations. If you have specific accessibility needs, please contact us before the event so we can ensure the best experience.

Still have questions?

Can't find the answer you're looking for? Please contact our support team.
